Its branches are long and before night fall you find the perfect place to rest your eyes. You feel your eyes shutgently and you drift off to sleep. In the middle of the night you wake up to a rapid scratching. Its coming from the bottom of the tree. So startled you feel yourself loose your grip. It stops and stares at you for a second. It tilts its head and begins circling you again. You feel your fingers letting up, do you?
