A Rainy Day

To begin with,the forest was sunny with not a cloud in sight.

John chased after his sister Sammy hastily. Dodging sticks and stones.

Sammy ran away from John, making her way toward their cabin in the woods.

Somehow, out of nowhere the clouds rolled in, and rain began to pour.

They were drenched, but only a meer few feet from safety.

When they reached the fireplace, they immediately put their hands in front of it, in order to warm their small cold hands.
