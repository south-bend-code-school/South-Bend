Description

"Through a challenging standard-based course of study, with educational decisions guided by data and policy. Riley students will become responsible learners who work collaboratively and accept ownership of their academic progress and social development."
- Riley's Mission Statement

Riley's magnet program includes a variety of engineering/technology programs, nationally certified through Project Lead the Way(PLTW).

PLTW is the leading provider of rigorous and innovative science, technology, engineering, and mathematics(STEM) curricular programs in the US.
Riley's PLTW certification allows students to earn dual credits and multiple scholarship opportunities only awarded to PLTW graduates!

Students in the Riley magnet programs create, design, build, discover, collaborate with peers and engineers, and solve problems while applying what they learn in math and science.

FaQs

What is the school’s educational philosophy?

  • ALL students are capable of learning and it is our responsibility to ensure that they do. Differentiated instruction maximizes student success. Students have a responsibility to be actively involved in the learning process. Students' educational opportunities are enriched through attending a shcool with a diverse population. Education is a lifelong process. Consistent school attendance is required for quality learning to occur. Each student is a valued individual with unique intellectual, physical, social, and emotional needs. Students, parent, staff, teachers, administrators, and the community share the responsibility and desire or educational excellence
