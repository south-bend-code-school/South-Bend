The Bakery

It was a sunny morning in the bakery, and it was the bakers birthday to be exact

The light peered in the small windw, on the edge of the corner.

Two kids were playing bashfully, in the kitchen of which they were not to be inside.

The baker found them angrily, he could smile at them, intead he looked at them with disgrace.

He chased them around the room with a broom.

They went out intoa prarie and had lunch.
