Pozole

how to make pozole

Materials and Ingredients: Chicken, big pot,strainer, Eposoti(HERB),Hominy, Water,Peppers, and a blender

Steps

STEP 1. First you fill the pot with water then place the chicken there and boil it.

STEP 2.After that Take the chicken out and split it up into stands and don't do anything with the chicken broth.

STEP 3.Once you are finished with splting the chicken into strands then you put it back and drop in the hominy and eposoti

STEP 4. While dong that you start to blend up the peppers in water

STEP 5.Then strain it through the strainer into the chicken broth tat contains all of those ingredients.

STEP 6.Lastly let it boil and then it's done.
