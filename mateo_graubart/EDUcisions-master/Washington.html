Description

"Washington’s pride is rooted in five tenants: preparedness, respect, integrity, discipline, and engagement."
- Washington's Mission Statement

Washington is one of four comprehensive high schools within the South Bend Community School Corporation and is proudly recognized for its rich athletic tradition and Medical Magnet Health Sciences program.

Washington is an AdvancEd accredited, public high school. All students can pursue a Core 40 or Academic or Technical Honors Diploma.
Magnet students receive broad-based preparation for careers in healthcare and scientific research through one of two program pathways.

All students can participate in diverse career exploration through career technical education or focus on rigorous advanced placement programming.

FaQs

What is the school’s educational philosophy?

  • ALL students are capable of learning and it is our responsibility to ensure that they do. Differentiated instruction maximizes student success. Students have a responsibility to be actively involved in the learning process. Students' educational opportunities are enriched through attending a shcool with a diverse population. Education is a lifelong process. Consistent school attendance is required for quality learning to occur. Each student is a valued individual with unique intellectual, physical, social, and emotional needs. Students, parent, staff, teachers, administrators, and the community share the responsibility and desire or educational excellence
